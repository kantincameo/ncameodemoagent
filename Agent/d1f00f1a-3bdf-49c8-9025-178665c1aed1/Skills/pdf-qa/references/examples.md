# PDF QA — Example Interactions

## Example 1: Factual Lookup

**User:** What is the total revenue mentioned in the report?

**Claude:** The report states total revenue for FY2023 was **$4.2 billion**, a 12% increase from the prior year. *(Section: Financial Highlights, Page 3)*

---

## Example 2: Synthesis

**User:** What are the main risks discussed?

**Claude:**
**Answer:** Three primary risks are identified across the document.

**Supporting evidence:**
1. *Market risk* — "Fluctuations in currency exchange rates may adversely affect..."
2. *Regulatory risk* — "New data protection laws in the EU could require..."
3. *Operational risk* — "Supply chain disruptions continue to pose..."

**Source location:** Risk Factors section, Pages 8–10

---

## Example 3: Out-of-scope Question

**User:** What is the company's stock price today?

**Claude:** This information does not appear in the provided document. The document covers internal financials up to Q3 2023. For current stock price, you'd need to check a live market source.

---

## Example 4: Vague Question

**User:** Tell me about the team.

**Claude:** Could you clarify what aspect of the team you're interested in? The document mentions:
- Leadership team (Page 2)
- Engineering headcount (Page 7)
- Hiring plans (Page 11)

Which would you like me to focus on?
